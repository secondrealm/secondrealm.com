Two ways to support artists w/o buying art.

DM an artwork to a friend who might like it

Write short tweet about an artwork. for example: "this artwork by @/name makes me feel [insert feeling]"

Tip: always include: one link, image, and @

1x a day each for min impact

A lot of people in this space are only interested in supporting if it’s beneficial to their trajectory and image. A large amount of interactions are transactional, it’s why if I’m in a big group chat I don’t share my posts because the culture of it all has become so toxic.

Artists that seemed to enjoy my work while i was in groupchats no longer show up after i left them a few months ago and that proves my point that 80% of gcs are just clicky and don't actually care about you

i’m seeing a lot of upset and frustrated artists here and i can empathize. i think it’s really important to manage your expectations especially in a space that cares more about profits than actual art appreciation. the abysmal climate has nothing to do with the quality of your art, so taking it personally is just needless suffering. if the effort you’re giving is unsustainable and unrequited, adjust your efforts to a level you are happy to sustain. i know this space preaches consistency and “showing up every day” but if that is detrimental to your career and mental health there is no shame in going elsewhere. if you’re exasperated by yelling into the void, then find somewhere that’s not a void. or learn to emotionally cope with the void if you choose to continue to participate at the same level. i’m at a place where i have art that hasn’t sold for over a year and i give zero fucks bc i’m playing the long game and participating in more art-focused communities outside of nfts. it just sucks to see artists who don’t feel appreciated or are upset by lack of sales. if you need to sell more to survive, diversify. web3 is currently not going to sustain most artists here. if you need more appreciation, find a community that appreciates art more. you don’t have to “leave nfts” to explore other communities that better serve you.

if you call it a marketplace I will cry we can do so much more than spend all this time selling let’s break free from this stale paradigm and focus on how the technology empowers us distribution at scale is its own marketing create art and distribute
