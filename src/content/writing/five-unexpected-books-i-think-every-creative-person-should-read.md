A curated list of five influential books spanning philosophy, business, productivity, and design that can shape how creative people think and work.

- *Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance* by Robert M. Pirsig

- *Setting the Table: The Transforming Power of Hospitality in Business* by Danny Meyer

- *The Power of When: Learn the Best Time to Eat Lunch, Ask for a Raise, Have Sex, Write a Novel, Take Your Meds, and More* by Michael Breus PhD

- *Deep Work: Rules for Focused Success in a Distracted World* by Cal Newport

- *The Design of Everyday Things* by Don Norman
