## Feeling like a slave to the algorithm? 

Christian Madsbjerg's "Sensemaking: The Power of the Humanities in the Age of the Algorithm" is a must-read for artists seeking to navigate the digital art world without losing the human connection. 

The book challenges the data-driven mentality that dominates today's artistic platforms and emphasizes the power of culture, language, and history in art appreciation. Madsbjerg's "Sensemaking" method offers practical principles to create art that resonates on a human level, despite the algorithmic landscape. 

An essential read for artists yearning to balance technology's conveniences with the timeless human elements of artistry.
