That is the moment when most people give up.

When the work feels too tough, the results seem too distant, and quitting seems like the easiest choice.

It's that crucial point where dreams often fade—not because they’re out of reach, but because they’re uncomfortable.

But here’s the twist: this is your chance.

It’s the point where others tap out, but you can decide to push through.

When you fight that urge to quit, you’re not just moving forward—you’re doing what most people won’t.
