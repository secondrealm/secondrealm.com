The guidelines below provide a framework through which I can make big decisions. I also use it recalibrate top priorities.

They have evolved slowly over the last 6 years since I first published them on Medium in 2018.

## My personal guidelines

### 🧘🏻 Philosophy

Life amplified by art (lifeᴬᴿᵀ)

### 📜 Motto

Semper Curiosa Semper Crescente  
(Always Curious, Always Growing)

### 🚀 Mission

- Create art.

### 👓 Vision

- Be my own boss.

### 💎 Principles

- Be consistent.  
- Be authentic.  
- Be gentle.  
- Be mindful.

> "There is always some kid who may be seeing me for the first or last time, I owe him my best." — Joe DiMaggio

### ❤️ My “Why”

Learn new things.

### ⚙️ Strategy

- Give space to mistakes.  
- Move in silence.  
- Go on quests.  
- Level up.  
- Read to understand.  
- Step into the fear.  
- Focus on “the one thing”.

> “The great aim of education is not knowledge but action” — Herbert Spencer

### 🏆Goal

- Make something that sticks to the ribs of our cultural consciousness.
