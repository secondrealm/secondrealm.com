Excited to share that I'm teaming up with Max Jackson, Empress Trash, and Patrick Amadon to set up an artist-centric organization in the Farcaster ecosystem. Our aim is to support artists by showcasing and preserving their work, creating a space for creativity to thrive. Starting with a shared vision to boost voices in cryptoart, we're building a platform for artists and collectors to connect and innovate. Together, we're all about transparency, inclusivity, and forward-thinking. This is more than just art displays; it's about empowering artists, respecting community values, and valuing every voice. Join us on this journey of ideas and art!
