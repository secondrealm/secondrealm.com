Choosing the right platform to mint your art can feel overwhelming, especially given how quickly on-chain technology evolves.

But if you're constantly jumping from one platform to the next without settling down, how can you expect collectors to connect with you?

Exploring different platforms is a natural part of finding the best fit for your work. I’m not saying don’t explore—absolutely, take the time to see what’s out there.

Dedicating yourself to one platform, at least for a while, can be far more beneficial for building a collector base.

Ultimately, the specific platform or chain you choose matters less if you’re spreading yourself too thin across too many places.

Every artist’s situation is unique, but consistency and a sense of home in one thing can make all the difference.
