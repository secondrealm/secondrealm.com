Hi, I'm Eric, artist, and founder of Second Realm that hails from the Land of Pizza and Bagels, New Jersey.

I've been building a passionate community of artists, collectors, supporters, and patrons of crypto art and NFTs since 2019. But I recently took time to evaluate my impact on the NFT landscape.

What follows is quantifiable data that I collected in an attempt to record what I’ve accomplished over the last two years. I won’t know exactly what these numbers mean until next year when I can measure them against each other. But based on what I’ve seen so far, I’m even more bullish on myself.

## NFT Customer Experience

I spent my career leading Customer Experience, Service Design, and Organizational Change efforts at tech companies. And I’ve been curious how my NFT business, which works at the intersection of art and tech, would compare to industry benchmark scores like NPS and CSAT. After sending out a survey to members of the NFT world, it seems quite clear that my customer-focused efforts are being noticed.

### 72 NPS Score

The tech industry Net Promoter Score benchmark is 60 (i.e., Apple's is 89). Above 80 is considered world-class. At 72, I’m so close!

### 85 CSAT Score

The current overall U.S. Customer Satisfaction Score benchmark is 74.4. At 85 we’re 10.6 pts above the benchmark. That’s fantastic!

### 131 Responses

131 members of the NFT community took the time and energy to provide feedback and commentary in under 24 hours.

### 91% Recommendation Rate

91% of collectors and investors would recommend Second Realm NFTs to their friends.

Source: 2021 Second Realm Customer Survey (as of July 15, 2021)

## NFT Market Creator

In February 2021 I tweeted out my custom punk which sent me down one of the most amazing NFT rabbit holes I’ve ever been down - my first avatar project, Unofficial Punks. And I am confident in saying, as a result, I ignited the alt-punks movement which led to the NFT avatar renaissance.

### The Community

Affectionatly called, The Punkverse, this community grew into an organic social movement and now there are over 150 sub-genres and 7000 Certified Unofficial Punks.

### The Result

We developed a dynamic artist-led micro-economy, and became the bridge between the OG collectible projects (i.e., CryptoPunks, Avastars, etc.) and the new avatar renaissance projects (i.e., Bored Ape Yacht Club, Cool Cats, etc.).

The all-time trading volume for the collective Punkverse is 5.5K ETH (~$17.1M) as of August 13, 2021. For perspective, this makes us a top 50 NFT project all-time for all categories on OpenSea.

Sources: Unofficial Punks Sub-genre List and The Short History of Alt-Punks NFTs
