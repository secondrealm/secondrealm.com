  A paperclip emoji is not a paperclip. It cannot bind pages, hold weight, or
  bend in your hand. It is a symbol standing in for an object, carrying meaning
  through recognition alone. In a world increasingly mediated by icons,
  interfaces, and signals, we often mistake representation for reality. This
  piece reflects on how simple symbols shape perception, memory, and meaning.
