Staying motivated as an artist can be challenging, especially when faced with self-doubt or creative blocks.

Steven Pressfield’s *Do the Work* is a powerful guide to overcoming these obstacles. The book underscores the importance of action and perseverance over the pursuit of “perfect ideas,” offering practical advice for navigating each stage of a creative project.
