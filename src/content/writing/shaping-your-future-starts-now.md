The past is a closed book, and the future is unwritten.

The only time that truly exists is right now.

Every decision you make today directly impacts what’s ahead.

Living in the present isn’t about forgetting the past or ignoring your vision for the future—it’s about staying focused and moving forward.

No matter where you spend your time—revisiting the past, focusing on the present, or imagining the future—you’re always shaping what’s to come.
