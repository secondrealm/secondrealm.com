My recent creative exploration is inspired by my academic studies in emotional intelligence. My aim is to improve my emotional vocabulary by associating emotions with colors, plants, and other symbols through a creative lens. At the moment, the research is ongoing and the output is still being considered. But here are my initial explorations into the first six emotions: Interest, Wonder, Confusion, Surprise, Awe, and Curiosity.
