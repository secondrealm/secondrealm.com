I’ve been open about the fact that I see therapists and coaches for the mental health benefits.

I’ve also been open about the fact that what led me to finding cryptoart in 2018 was that I was rediscovering the healing powers of art for myself.

In fact my Brutalist Mannequin series and the new Life is Gray series are examples of how art as a therapeutic tool can manifest itself for me.

In my latest interview with Brady Evan Walker of Makersplace I discuss art as therapy in more depth than ever before. Check it out! 👇🏼
