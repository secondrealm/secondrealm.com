Over the last year, my newsletter became more of a long-form blog. But that’s not what I promised when I launched it a few years ago.

## My promise.

You can expect occasional updates on new artwork, upcoming drops, exhibitions, and developments in my art career only. I will be as efficient as possible with your time. So each newsletter will be a short 2-min read with one image and one hyperlink. And never be more than 1x a week but probably much less.

Going forward, that’s what you can expect here.

I’ll continue writing Eric’s Creative Bytes. You can find all those posts on my website.

Thanks for being on this journey with me!
