The secret sauce to staying ahead isn’t a secret and isn't about talent—it’s about the grind.

Those small actions, the ones that don’t take any special skill, when done savagely, are what set you apart.

Being reliable, showing up, paying attention to detail, and committing to quality every single time is the bedrock of success, a foundation that talent alone can’t match.

Talent can open doors, but that consistent focus on executing the fundamentals keeps you in the room and compounds over time.

Are you bringing intensity to the things that take no talent?
