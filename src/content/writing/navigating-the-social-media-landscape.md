Some of you may know there are dozens and dozens of web3 social media products all promising connection with your audience. I read somewhere there were 53 of them. Of the ones that are working to be Twitter’s competitor, I was exploring Blue Sky, Mastadon, Nostr, and Farcaster. But that’s too many for me to keep track of while maintaining an honest connection with my audience. So I've decided to mostly focus on Twitter. I'll continue exploring the others, but my primary communication channel will be there. You can confirm my Web3 social accounts here!
