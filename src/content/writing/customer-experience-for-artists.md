Collectors are customers & dealing with customers can be annoying. (they are NOT always right) Still, it's wise to think about how we speak about them in public. Potential customers see this. Handle it how you think it should be handled, just think about it.

Manage expectations. You don't HAVE to be available & engaged with your audience 24/7 as a business owner, artist or creator. (unless you want to be) Set the tone early on. Let them know what you expect from them AND what they can expect from you.

embrace the haterade. sometimes it gets you more awareness than people just showing you "the love" all the time.
